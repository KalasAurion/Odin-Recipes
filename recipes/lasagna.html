Lasagna Recipe

Description

The classic lasagna, a delicious way to feed the family. Who doesn't love layers of pasta, cheese, tomato sauce, and meat? Perfect fresh or as leftovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 one onion, diced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 28 ounces tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 14 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp oregano, dried
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/8 tsp each of basil, rosemary, and thyme (dried)
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • Salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles (9 unbroken)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 24 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 4 ounces parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. This will be for the pasta. Prepare the sauce (next step) while the water is coming to boil.
  2. Heat the olive oil on medium-high heat in a large skillet. Add the ground beef and cook until lightly browned on all sides. Remove the beef from the heat and reserve 1 Tbsp of fat.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and onions to the skillet and cook for around 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook 30 seconds.Return the cooked beef to the skillet and cook for around 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer the contents of the skillet to a medium-sized pot. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to the pot. Add the parsley, oregano, and other italian herbs, adjusting to taste. Sprinkle the pinch of garlic powder to taste. Add red wine vinegar and sugar, adjusting sugar to taste as needed. Add salt and bring to a simmer. Once simmering lower heat to maintain a simmer. Cook for 20-40 minutes, stirring often so nothing sticks to the bottom of the pot. Remove from heat.
  5. Add the dry noodles to the boiling water from step 1 and cook them per package directions. Stir often to prevent from sticking. Drain into a colander and rinse with cool water, gently seperating noodles that are stuck together. Spread a small amount of olive oil on a large rimmed baking sheet and lay out the cooked noodles, turning them over so that they get coated on both sides with oil. This will keep them from sticking together.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. In a 9 x 13 in casserole or lasagna dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish. Arrange one layer of noodles lenghtwise over the sauce. Ladle more sauce over the noodles. Sprinkle a layer (about 1/3) of the mozzarella over the sauce. Add half of the ricotta cheese; sprinkle half of the grated parmesan cheese over the top of the ricotta. Add a second layer of noodles and top it with half of the remaining sauce. Add half of the remaining mozzarella, the rest of the ricotta cheese, and the rest of the parmesan. Finish with another layer of noodles and spread the remaining sauce over them; top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover the lasagna with aluminium, tented slightly, and back for 45 minutes. Uncover during the last 5 minutes to brown the cheese and edges slightly.
  9. Allow the lasagna to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving
